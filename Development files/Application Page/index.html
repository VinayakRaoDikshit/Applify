FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I check the status of my application? You can check the status of your application by referring to the 'Status' column on the landing page. It will indicate whether your application is pending, under review, or has been processed.
Can I import/export my application data? Yes, you can import/export your application data by using the 'Export/Import Data' feature in the sidebar. This allows you to backup or transfer your application information easily.
What are 'Summary Insights'? 'Summary Insights' provide a summarized overview of your job applications. It includes statistics such as the number of applications submitted, the most common job profiles applied for, and other useful insights to help you track your progress.
